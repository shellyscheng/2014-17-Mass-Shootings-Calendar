1439 Days. 1317 Mass Shootings. Zero Action from Congress.

by Shiying Cheng  |  December 10, 2017

There have been 273 mass shootings in 2014, 333 in 2015, 383 in 2016, and 328 in 2017 so far. Since Jan. 1, 2014, at least 1,513 people have been killed and 5,693 have been injured in mass shootings.



Data Source: Gun Violence Archive (Last updated: Dec 10, 2017)
